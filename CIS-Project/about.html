What is Feedie?

s

What is Feedie? Have you ask yourself "What is good around here?" and to only be stumped with the realization that you don't know the area well enough and are unable to answer the question. Well this is what Feedie is designed to do. Feedie is a website dedicated to find anything about or unique events about food pertaining Arizona. Our goal is to share our love of food to with everyone around Arizona. So join us and our adventure to explore the unique thing Arizona have to offer us!
